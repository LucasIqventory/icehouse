# Layout Principles

The trailer is always the hero.

Ice is the atmosphere.

Never let decorative effects overpower the product.

Every page should feel:

Cold

Sharp

Industrial

Purpose-Built

Confident

Avoid:

* Rounded SaaS cards
* Generic landing-page layouts
* Holiday imagery
* Cartoon snowflakes
* Excessive animations

The site should feel like a piece of equipment forged from ice.
